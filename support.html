Frequently asked questions

How is my dog's calorie target calculated?

FitPaws uses the WSAVA/NRC veterinary formula: RER (Resting Energy Requirement) = 70 × weight(kg)⁰·⁷⁵, multiplied by a K-factor based on your dog's age, neutered status, and breed. The result is adjusted for your dog's activity level. Always check with your vet for dogs with medical conditions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → FitPaws and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep premium access until the end of the billing period. We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf as billing is managed by Apple.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data (dog profiles, food logs, weight history) within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first through your device's subscription settings.

Can I use FitPaws for multiple dogs?

The Family plan supports up to 5 dog profiles. The free and Companion plans support one dog profile.

Is FitPaws a replacement for veterinary advice?

No. FitPaws provides nutritional guidance based on established veterinary formulas, but it is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice. Always consult your vet for dogs with health conditions, unusual weight changes, or specific dietary needs.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link from noreply@fitpaws.app — check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
