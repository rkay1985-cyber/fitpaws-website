Features

Everything your dog needs to thrive

From daily food tracking to AI-powered nutrition coaching — all in one place.

🎯

Personalised calorie targets

Daily targets calculated using WSAVA veterinary formulas, adjusted for your dog's breed, age, neutered status, and activity level.

🍗

Food logging

Search thousands of pet foods, scan barcodes, or add your own. Track calories, portions, and meal frequency with ease.

⚖️

Weight tracking

Log weight over time and visualise trends. Set a goal weight and track progress toward it week by week.

🏃

Activity logging

Record walks, runs, swims, and play sessions. See how activity affects daily calorie needs in real time.

🤖

AI Nutrition Coach

Ask nutrition questions and get personalised advice based on your dog's actual logged data, breed, and health goals.

📋

Vet-ready reports

Generate professional health reports covering nutrition, weight trends and activity history — ready to share at your next vet visit.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

1

Create your dog's profile

Enter breed, age, weight, and activity level. SaluPaws calculates a personalised calorie target instantly.

2

Log meals daily

Search our food database, scan a barcode, or add a custom food. Each entry updates the daily calorie tracker.

3

Track weight & activity

Weigh in regularly and log exercise. See how it all fits together in the daily dashboard.

4

Ask the Coach

Have a nutrition question? The AI Coach answers based on your dog's actual data — not generic advice.
